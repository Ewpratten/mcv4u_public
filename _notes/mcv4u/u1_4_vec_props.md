The following are true for all vectors:

$$
\begin{aligned}
    \vec{a} + \vec{b} &= \vec{b} + \vec{a}\\
    (\vec{a} + \vec{b}) + \vec{c} &= \vec{a} + (\vec{b} + \vec{c})\\
    \vec{a} + \vec{0} &= \vec{a} \\
    k ( \vec{a} + \vec{b}) &= k \vec{a} + k \vec{b}\\
    m(n \vec{a}) &= (mn) \vec{a} = mn \vec{a}\\
    (m + n) \vec{a} &= m \vec{a} + n \vec{a}\\
\end{aligned}
$$
